Welcome to VolTech

VolTech is a student-run robotics team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge since 2021. Our six members work across mechanical, software, and business departments, building robots and managing our nonprofit organization. No experience is needed to join—just a willingness to learn and be part of the team.

We build robots and a whole lot more.

Grow as leaders while building teamwork and project management experience

Secure sponsorships and market the team through entrepreneurial strategies

Foster a dynamic, hands-on space for curiosity and collaboration

Encourage originality, critical thinking, and inventive solutions
